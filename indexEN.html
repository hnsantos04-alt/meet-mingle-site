Speakers

Moisés Marques

Human Resources executive with over 30 years of international experience across regions such as Latin America, North America, Europe, and Asia. With a background in leading multinational companies, he is a specialist in leadership, organizational culture, and transformation. He holds degrees from FGV and London Business School and brings a practical approach to topics such as inclusion and belonging in his talks, inspiring more human and high-performance work environments.

Leandro Iuamoto

Medical doctor graduated from USP, with additional training at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, he works at the intersection of health, leadership, and human development. Author of the book “The Science of Ikigai,” he brings to his talks practical reflections on self-awareness, purpose, and high performance, with an inspiring approach focused on transforming individuals and organizations.
